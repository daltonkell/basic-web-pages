Extras

This page is about miscellaneous HTML things, but you may be interested in links or images. The relative paths provided in the href (<a href="../images.html">images</a>) are relative to the current directory -- so make sure you've got your directories in order!
In fact, all the images in this tutorial will be loaded with relative URLs. This allows us to rename any of our chapter slugs (what's a slug?) without having to update all our image paths.
